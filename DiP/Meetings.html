Annual General Meetings (AGMs)

2019 Meeting

The first AGM of Executive Body Members; agenda: Forming the following Houses: KhoiSan House, House of Intequinism, Christian Afrikaans House, English House, Zulu House, Xhosa House, Sotho House, Tsonga House, Tswana House and Venda House.

If Executive Body Members want to table other proposals, they must do, in accordance with the constitution.

On 16 March the above Houses were formed. No additional proposals were tabled.

2020 Meeting

Decisions for 2020 meeting:

(1) Forming a Multiculturalist House, House of Independents and Muslim House.

(2) Divided Party will change its name on the next general meeting to include the words "Federal Party", because the meaning of divide, in English, does not have the same meaning, "verdeel" (divide) to "deel" (share) has in Afrikaans.
