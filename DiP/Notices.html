Notices

By-Elections

Anyone who wants to contest by-elections on behalf of Divided Party (DiP) should contact DiP, to make arrangements. The list of by-elections gives dates, municipalities and wards where by-elections will be held.

New Name

Divided Party will change its name on the next general meeting to include the words "Federal Party", because the meaning of divide, in English, does not have the same meaning, "verdeel" (divide) to "deel" (share) has in Afrikaans.
